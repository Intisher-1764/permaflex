A complete lineup of high-performance coating and sealing materials designed for professional results.

Permaflex

Permaflex is a one-part, thick liquid rubber coating designed as a high-adhesion primer and topcoat. It applies like paint but cures into a durable, flexible rubber layer that permanently bonds to concrete, wood, steel, masonry, and similar building materials.

Liquid Rubber Base (LRB)

LRB is a single-component liquid rubber that cures completely throughout its thickness in under 4 hours, forming a solid flexible rubber compound similar in toughness to a truck tire. Ideal for cracks, joints, holes, divots, and damaged concrete areas.

TAV (Thickening Activator)

TAV is used with LRB to thicken the mix for vertical, angled, or overhead repairs, so it stays in place without sagging.
